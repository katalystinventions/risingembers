The Flames Beckon

In the world of Rising Embers: Book 1, an ancient spark awakens, forging alliances out of ashes and shaping heroes from humble hearts. Shadows loom, yet faint embers glow—calling you to follow the soft crackle of fate. Step forward, traveler, into a dance of light and dark.

Rising Embers: Book 1 follows a determined protagonist who sparks a revolution in a world teetering on the edge of darkness. As hidden flames stir in distant realms, friendships ignite, secrets smolder, and destinies burn bright. Journey through treacherous lands, dance with unspeakable danger, and discover the power that glows deep within every heart—waiting for the moment to blaze.
